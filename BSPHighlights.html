AAA Battery Service Program

Timing is Everything

The Warranty Clock

PURCHASE DATE TIMELINES IMPORTANT
  • Beginning 2023: AAA batteries purchased on or after Jan 1, 2023 have a 3-year limited free replacement warranty.
  • Before 2023: Batteries purchasedbefore Jan 1, 2023, include a 3-year free replacement during years 1-3 PLUS a prorated discount if replaced during years 4–6.
  • Warranty Restarts: The warranty for AAA batteries replaced during years 1-3 (free replacement window) does NOT restart the clock; it follows the OPD.

Agent Skillshot

"Mrs. Langford purchased a AAA battery Jan 5, 2023. It failed in April 2026. Can we offer free replacement?" (Yes/No)

WILL THE CALL COUNT?

Call Count Rules

New Purchases

New AAA battery purchases at roadside ALWAYS count against the member's annual call allotment.

Warranty Service

When a AAA battery is replaced under warranty (test bad + replaced) the call does NOT count against annual allotment.

[Service Charge will be refunded, if applicable.]
Service Charge

Calls Available = 0

Members with 0 calls available must pay Service Charge upfront. If the battery fails testing and is replaced under warranty, the Service Charge WILL be refunded.
