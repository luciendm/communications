Battery Service Program (BSP)
BSP technicians test the battery and electrical system to diagnose issues and determine next steps.
What’s Included
AAA batteries include a 3-year free replacement warranty. Installation applies to most passenger vehicles and RVs, but excludes motorcycles and similar vehicles.
Receipts & Documentation
Members receive a digital or printed receipt. If lost, they can contact the provider or a CMS case can be created for follow-up.
Warranty Rules
The 3-year warranty does not restart after replacement. Older batteries may follow prorated rules depending on purchase date.
Availability & Edge Cases
If the correct battery is unavailable, a jumpstart may be provided. Non-members must visit a BSPAAR facility for warranty service.
